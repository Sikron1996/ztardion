How to mint a ZTARDIO

STEP 1
Create a Zcash wallet that supports transparent addresses (t-address).
STEP 2
Deposit $ZEC from any exchange or wallet to your Zcash wallet.
STEP 3
Choose how many NFTs you want to mint and send the exact amount of ZEC from your wallet.
